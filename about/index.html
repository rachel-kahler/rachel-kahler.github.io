About Rachel Kahler

I am an Electrical Engineering student at the University of Texas at Dallas. I am a part of the Collegium V Honors college, and am a National Merit Scholarship awardee. I run DIII cross country for the university, and am a part of the Society of Women Engineers. Additonally, I am part of a Latin Dance club called "Stop Pretending You Can't Dance". I am passionate about Martian weather and space exploration! My Mars research has won me numerous awards from IEEE, and I was a keynote speaker for the Miami Valley Astronomical Society.
